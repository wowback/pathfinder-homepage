Privacy Policy

PATHFINDER Inc. (hereinafter "Company") establishes and discloses this Privacy Policy in accordance with Article 30 of the Personal Information Protection Act to protect the personal information of data subjects and to handle related grievances promptly and smoothly.

Article 1 (Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information)

The Company collects and uses personal information for the following purposes. Collected personal information will not be used for purposes other than those stated below, and if the purpose of use changes, necessary measures such as obtaining separate consent will be taken.

① Business inquiry reception and processing: Reviewing inquiry content, responding, and handling follow-up tasks

② Service improvement: Understanding site usage and improving service quality

Article 2 (Personal Information Items Collected and Collection Methods)

① Items collected

  • Required: Contact name, company name, email address
  • Optional: Phone number
  • Automatically collected: IP address, access date and time, browser type

② Collection methods

  • Direct input by users through the inquiry form on the Site
  • Automatic collection upon site access (cookies, server logs)

Article 3 (Retention and Use Period of Personal Information)

① The Company destroys personal information without delay once the purpose of collection and use has been achieved. However, where retention is required by applicable laws, the information is retained as follows.

  • Records related to contracts or withdrawal of offers: 5 years (E-Commerce Act)
  • Records related to consumer complaints or dispute resolution: 3 years (E-Commerce Act)
  • Records related to access: 3 months (Protection of Communications Secrets Act)

② Personal information collected through inquiries is retained for 1 year after the inquiry has been processed, then destroyed.

Article 4 (Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties)

① The Company processes personal information only within the scope specified in Article 1 and does not, in principle, provide it to third parties.

② Exceptions apply in the following cases.

  • When the data subject has given prior consent
  • When required by law or requested by investigative authorities in accordance with legally prescribed procedures

Article 5 (Destruction of Personal Information)

① The Company destroys personal information without delay when it becomes unnecessary, such as when the retention period has expired or the processing purpose has been achieved.

② Methods of destruction are as follows.

  • Electronic files: Permanently deleted using methods that prevent recovery
  • Paper documents: Shredded or incinerated

Article 6 (Rights and Obligations of Data Subjects and How to Exercise Them)

① Data subjects may exercise the following rights against the Company at any time.

  • Request to access personal information
  • Request to correct or delete personal information
  • Request to suspend processing of personal information
  • Withdrawal of consent to processing of personal information

② Rights may be exercised by contacting us via email at info@pathfinderinc.co, and the Company will take action without delay.

③ If a data subject requests correction or deletion of personal information due to errors, the Company will not use or provide the relevant information until correction or deletion is complete.

Article 7 (Measures to Ensure Safety of Personal Information)

The Company takes the following measures to ensure the safety of personal information.

① Administrative measures: Minimizing personnel handling personal information and providing training

② Technical measures: Managing access rights to personal information processing systems, installing and operating security programs

③ Physical measures: Access control for locations where personal information is stored

Article 8 (Operation and Refusal of Cookies)

① The Company may use cookies to provide customized services to users.

② Users have the right to choose whether to accept cookies and may allow or refuse cookies through browser settings.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data

③ Refusing cookie installation may cause inconvenience in using some services.

Article 9 (Privacy Officer)

  • Name: Sungwook Baek

Article 10 (Changes to Privacy Policy)

① This Privacy Policy may be supplemented, deleted, or revised due to changes in laws, policies, or security technology.

② Any changes will be announced on the Site, and amendments will take effect from the date of announcement.
